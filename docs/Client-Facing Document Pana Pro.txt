### **Project Management Methodology: Agile MVP with MoSCoW Prioritization**

We use a flexible, results-driven approach to build quickly and adapt:

- **MVP Focus:** Start with essentials (core ordering and sales tools) for rapid value, then layer enhancements.
- **Weekly Sprints:** 1-week cycles—plan, build, review, refine. You'll see progress via simple demos.
- **MoSCoW Prioritization:** We categorize features to keep things focused:
    - **Must Have:** Non-negotiables (e.g., product catalog upload).
    - **Should Have:** High-value adds (e.g., order tracking dashboard).
    - **Could Have:** Polish items (e.g., blog integration).
    - **Won't Have:** Future-phase ideas (e.g., advanced reporting).

This ensures we're aligned, with your feedback shaping each step. Weekly reviews: A one-page summary of wins, previews, and quick polls (e.g., "Does this preview tool feel intuitive?").
